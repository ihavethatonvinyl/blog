I Have that on Vinyl is now a year and a half old. 300 essays, 150 pieces from me, 60 interviews. I published pieces at a frenetic pace for a year, then slowed down to one a day, then to every other day. And now IHTOV changes again. 

Starting now, IHTOV will be a monthly publication. Each issue will consist of about three guest essays and a piece by me. Sometimes there may be an interview thrown in there. This pace will be much better for maintaining IHTOV, better for the readers, and better for me in general. 

I will still strive to bring you the most interesting takes on vinyl and music, written by people who are passionate about their subject matter and the music they listen to. I will still bring you stories that are poignant, emotional, funny, and entertaining. 

Thanks for sticking with IHTOV through its growing pains and changes. 

And please, still send me your pitches. I would love to help tell your story.
